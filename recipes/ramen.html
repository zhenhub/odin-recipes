Ramen

Description:

Take the usual ramen up a notch with this quick homemade ramen. Fresh veggies and herbs make this extra delicious, healthy, and cozy!

Ingredients:

  • Instant noodles
  • Sesame oil
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Chicken broth
  • Dried shiitake mushrooms
  • Scallions
  • Kale
  • Carrots
  • Siracha
  • Panko crumbs
  1. Heat the sesame oil in a large skillet
  2. Add the garlic and ginger to stir fry for 2 minutes
  3. Add the chicken broth and bring it to a simmer
  4. Add the mushrooms and simmer until softened
  5. Add instant noodles for 5 minutes
  6. Top off with scallions, kale and carrots
